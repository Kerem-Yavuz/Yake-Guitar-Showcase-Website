About

Yake Guitars is a small business located in Istanbul, Turkey. We specialize in handcrafted guitars made with the highest quality materials and craftsmanship. Our guitars are designed to be both beautiful and functional, and we are committed to providing our customers with a truly unique and personal experience. We offer a wide variety of guitars to choose from, including electric and bass guitars. We also offer custom-made guitars to meet your specific needs. Our guitars are perfect for beginners, experienced players, and anyone who loves the sound of a great guitar. We believe that every guitar should have its own unique sound. That's why we take the time to handcraft each guitar with care. We use only the finest materials and craftsmanship to ensure that each guitar produces a beautiful, rich sound.

You can contact us via our Instagram Account. We are always happy to answer your questions and help you find the perfect guitar for your needs.
